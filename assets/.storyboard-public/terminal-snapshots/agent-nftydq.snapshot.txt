  4 Key Principles for Sustainable Design Systems

  domain stewards outside the core team to prevent bottlenecks.

  3. Versioning & the Breaking Change Contract — Semantic versioning with explicit
  policies + automated codemods turns upgrades from projects into routine maintenance.

  4. Adoption Metrics Over Usage Metrics — Track adoption rate and drift rate, not raw
  usage counts. Drift = the system isn't meeting real needs; fix before building new
  components.
